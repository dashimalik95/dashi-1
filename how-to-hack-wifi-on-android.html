Wifi Hacking

Gaining access to someone's wifi by cracking the password of the wifi is known as Wifi Hacking. Wifi Hacking is very common these days. There are many techniques to hack wifi. Normally wifi is hacked through Kali Linux or other systems but it can also be hacked through an Android.


Requirements To Hack Wifi On Android

1. Wifi Manager (You can download Wifi Manager by searching it in your Google Play Store.)



Method To Hack Wifi On Android

1. Download Wifi Manager.

2. Open Wifi Manager.

3. Click on Update.

4. Now, click on the network that you want to hack.

5. Now, it will prompt you to enter the password of the wifi.

6. On the same screen, you will see something called "BSS". The numbers written after BSS are very important.

7. Skip the 1st four number of BSS and enter the remaining numbers as the password (Don't include colon : in it).

8. When you enter the remaining numbers as the password, remember to change the last number of the remaining numbers by reducing it by one. For eg. If the number is "12345678". You will write it as "12345677".

9. This is the password. Enjoy!
