Tracking

Tracking can be done by many means. Mobile number can be tracked and CNIC number also can be tracked.


Requirements To Track Mobile Number

1. Person Tracker Pro (You can download Person Tracker Pro by clicking here).



Method To Track Mobile Number

1. Download Person Tracker Pro.

2. Open Person Tracker Pro.

3. As, this app cannot be used without credits so first off all you have to earn some credits. Therefore you should click on Earn Credits.

4. After you click on Earn Credits, an Ad will play and you will get credits.

5. Now you have to click on the particular option that you want to use. There are three options:

● Find Name
● Find CNIC
● Find Address

6. After clicking on the particular option, you will be asked to enter the mobile number of the person.

7. After entering the mobile number, you will get the details. Enjoy!
