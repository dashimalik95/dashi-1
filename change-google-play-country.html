Change Google Play Country





Note: This is just for educational purposes. Do not misuse.



Google Play

The app store for Android Operating System is known as Google Play. It was developed by Google on March 6, 2012. It has millions of users in the world. It distributes apps according to countries, so you might have problem if an app that you want isn't available in your country and for that reason, you may want to make any other country Google Play Account. Another reason is that if you have moved to a new country you may want to switch to that country play store. Read below to know how to Change Google Play Country.



Method to Change Google Play Country

1. Open Google Play Store.

2. Now, slide the menu on the left side.

3. Now, click on Account.

4. Now, if you have access to change your country, you will see a Country and Profiles option in this menu.

5. Now, click on the Country and Profiles option.

6. Now, select your new country.

7. If a warning prompt appears, click on yes.

8. Congratulations, you are done. You have successfully changed google play country.
