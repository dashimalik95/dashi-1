Privacy Policy


We respect the privacy of our visitors and following are the details on what type of data we collect when you visit our website. We never sell your personal information to anyone.


Just like other blogs and website, when you visit our site your log file that contains the following data is collected:

  • Your IP Address
  • ISP Provider
  • Your Browser
  • Time of Visit
  • Country you are visiting from

We DONT collect and save your email address unless you opt to contact us via the contact form.


Advertisement


We also use third party advertisements on Andrify to support our site. Some of these advertisers may use technology such as cookies and web beacons when they advertise on our site, which will also send these advertisers (such as Google through the Google AdSense program) information including your IP address, your ISP, the browser you used to visit our site, and in some cases, whether you have Flash installed. This is generally used for geotargeting purposes (showing New York real estate ads to someone in New York) or showing certain ads based on specific sites visited (such as showing cooking ads to someone who frequents cooking sites).

You can choose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs such as AntiVirus or Internet Security. However, this can affect how you are able to interact with our site as well as other websites. This could include the inability to login to services or programs, such as logging into forums or accounts.

We can assure you that you can put your trust in our site and visit it without any hesitation.
